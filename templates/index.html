About the Game

The Final Harvest was a game that ran from Jan 9 - 27 where each player had a custom link that they tried to get as many people to click as they could. After almost three weeks, thousands of players from around the world got thousands more to click their links, and in the end, the winner scored over 3,000. Congratulations, brayn! The game was developed in three days for Ludum Dare 52. You can still share a link if you want...
